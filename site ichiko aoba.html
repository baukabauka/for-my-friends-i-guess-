Ichiko Aoba

Who is she????

Ichiko Aoba is a Japanese folk singer and songwriter. Her main instrument is guitar, on which she composes most of her music.

Some basic informating about her
  • Born 28 January 1990
  • Shes 33 years old
  • From Kyoto prefecture in Japan
  • Genres: Folk Music
  • Singer and Songwriter
